About this Experimental Study
In this experiment you will be asked to perform a fruit-picking shopping task and then to complete a questionnaire. When picking the fruits please pay attention to:

 1. You will have to choose 10 different fruit types from the given list.
 2. A list of alternatives for the same fruit will be presented to you.
  You can only choose one fruit per type.
 3. Once you confirm your selection of a fruit, it can't be undone.
 4. You'll have to choose the fruits within a budget of 20 euros
 5. Please make eco-friendly decisions, while staying within the specified budget.
 6. If at any point in time, you decide that you not longer wish to participate,.
  please press the "STOP" button.
